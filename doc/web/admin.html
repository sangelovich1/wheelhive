/channels_add

Add a channel for the bot to analyze.

/channels_add channel:#your-channel category:community

Parameters:

  • channel - Select from dropdown menu
  • category - Choose based on channel purpose:
    • community - Member discussions, trade ideas, strategies, position sharing, Q&A, sentiment. Bot analyzes these for trading patterns, popular tickers, and community consensus.
    • news - Market news feeds, earnings announcements, economic reports, stock-specific news. Bot uses these to understand market context and connect news events to trading activity.
üí° Category Purpose:
‚Ä¢ Community channels show WHAT the community is trading
‚Ä¢ News channels show WHY (market catalysts, events, context)
‚Ä¢ Together, they give the AI a complete picture for better insights
‚úÖ Success Message:
Channel Added

Messages will be analyzed for trading insights
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: The bot must have Read Messages and Read Message History permissions in the channel. The command will warn you if permissions are missing.
/channels_remove

Stop analyzing a specific channel.

/channels_remove channel:#old-channel
When to use:
  • Channel is no longer active
  • Channel repurposed for non-trading topics
  • Want to reduce bot activity

üí° FAQ Management

Build a custom knowledge base for your server's AI assistant with server-specific trading strategies and guidelines.

/faq_list

View all FAQs in your server's knowledge base.

/faq_list
Shows each FAQ with:
  • Question and answer preview
  • Who added it
  • FAQ ID (for removal)
/faq_add

Add a new FAQ to your knowledge base with AI validation.

/faq_add

How it works:

  1. Command opens a form with two fields:
    • Question (200 chars max)
    • Answer (2000 chars max)
  2. AI validates quality (must score ‚â•70%)
  3. If approved, immediately available to AI assistant
‚úÖ Good FAQ Example:

Q: When should I roll a covered call that's deep in-the-money?

A: Roll a deep ITM covered call when:
1. Before expiration: Roll 7-14 days before expiry
2. Price threshold: Stock is 5-10% above call strike
3. Next expiry: Roll to next monthly at higher strike
4. Credit requirement: Ensure net credit received

Example: Stock at $57, sold $50 call ‚Üí buy back and sell next month's $58 call
‚ùå Poor FAQ Example:

Q: How do options work?
A: Options give you the right to buy or sell.

Why it fails: Too generic, incomplete, not wheel-specific
/faq_remove

Delete a FAQ by its ID.

/faq_remove faq_id:faq_850508_2025-11-12_abc123

Get the FAQ ID from /faq_list

‚ö° Quick Start Guide

First-Time Setup (5 minutes)

  1. Run /channels_add channel:#trading-chat category:community
  2. Run /channels_add channel:#market-news category:news
  3. Run /channels_list to verify

  6. Click Save Changes

üìö Best Practices

Understanding Channel Categories

üí¨ Community Channels

Purpose: Capture what your community is actually trading and thinking

What the bot extracts:

  • Trade entries (options, shares) from text messages
  • Sentiment about specific tickers (bullish/bearish)
  • Popular trading strategies being discussed
  • Position updates and trade results
  • Questions and concerns (risk indicators)

Best channels to add:

  • #trading-chat - General discussion
  • #my-trades - Position sharing
  • #options-strategy - Strategy discussion
  • #daily-plays - Day-to-day activity
üì∞ News Channels

Purpose: Provide market context so AI understands WHY trades are happening

What the bot uses news for:

  • Connect trading activity to market events
  • Understand catalysts behind ticker mentions
  • Identify earnings plays vs regular trades
  • Track economic data impact on sentiment
  • Provide richer context in AI-generated insights

Best channels to add:

  • #market-news - Bloomberg, WSJ, Reuters feeds
  • #earnings-calendar - Upcoming reports
  • #economic-data - Fed meetings, CPI, jobs reports
  • #stock-alerts - Price alerts, unusual activity
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: News channels should contain actual market news, not server announcements or admin posts. Use Discord webhooks or RSS bots to feed real market news into these channels.

Channel Configuration Tips

  • ‚úÖ Review monthly: Run /channels_list to audit active channels
  • ‚úÖ Configure permissions first: Grant bot access before adding channel
  • ‚úÖ Balance both categories: Aim for 60-70% community, 30-40% news channels
  • ‚úÖ Quality over quantity: Better to have 3-4 active channels than 10 quiet ones
  • ‚ùå Don't add: Private channels, NSFW channels, off-topic channels, server announcements

FAQ Management

  • ‚úÖ Focus on wheel strategy: Position management, rolling, strikes
  • ‚úÖ Be specific: Include examples and step-by-step guidance
  • ‚úÖ Server-specific rules: Position sizing, risk management guidelines
  • ‚úÖ Keep updated: Remove outdated FAQs regularly
  • ‚ùå Don't duplicate: Bot already knows general options concepts
  • ‚ùå Avoid generic info: "What is delta?" - already in base knowledge
